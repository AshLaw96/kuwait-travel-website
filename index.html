Kuwait is a must country to visit in the Middle East. It might not be one of the most well-known travel destinations, but I'm going to show you why it should be a travel destination for you. With its beautiful architecture, stunning natural wonders, and modern Arabic feel, Kuwait is more than a desert haven.

Accommodations

There is an abundance of places to stay in Kuwait whether it's luxury you want or a family-oriented stay, here are just a few of them.

  • Waldorf Astoria Kuwait

    • The Waldorf has 200 lavish rooms and suites to choose from, with 5 mouth-watering restaurants, a luxurious spa, an outdoor pool, and an exclusive club. If fine dining and 5-star lodgings is what your looking for this may be the place for you!

  • Marriott Executive Apartments

    • The Marriott has one-, two-, and three-bedroom studio apartments, with your own kitchenette and dinning area. If you prefer a more homely like feel but with the 5-star standers then look no further than the Marriott!

  • Hilton Kuwait Resort

    • With a variety of comfortable rooms for all the family, or perhaps a villa with your own private pool. The Hilton Resort will be a perfect getaway for you to explore!

  • Sama Aljeyad

    • This entire home in Abdali includes everything you need for you and your family to enjoy. The Sama Aljeyad even has its own football pitch and many other incredible activities for you to make the most amazing memories!

  • Flowery Holiday Suites

    • This boutique accommodation has a variety of unique spacious rooms at a budget. The hotel has its very own terrace so you can view all the beautiful glimmering lights of Kuwait City at night. If your looking for a stay that is a little bit quirky but at a reasonable price, this is a perfect choice!

Activities

There are a variety of things to do in Kuwait. If you're an adrenaline junkie or you are looking for the relaxing venture, these are just some of them.

  • Kuwait Motor Town

    • If the fast life is what you're looking for, then this is the place for you. With Off-roading and track racing, it has everything you motor heads can dream of.

  • Wibit water park

    • If you love the water then this might be right adventure for you. This massive inflatable has something for all ages, with a kids area located in the shallower end, a place of tranquil relaxation for those that want to chill at sea, and the main obstacle course that will test your balance and agility whilst making some unforgettable memories.

  • Marina World

    • With over 150 shops the Marina Mall has everything you need to shop until you drop. With some of the best brands around the world, located along Kuwait's coastline, you can admire the stunning Gulf whilst perusing all of your favorite brands.

  • The Grand Mosque

    • For you culture lovers, The Grand Mosque is a must visit - with its eye-catching architecture, rich history and pure greatness. You can find out a multitude of interesting facts on the Kuwaiti culture from the very knowledgeable members of staff or just gaze at the extravagant mosaics around the massive structure.

Cuisines

Kuwait's food is a multicultural mix of Arabian, Iranian, Indian, and Mediterranean delight. With this wonderful mix of tastes you'll never want to leave this Eastern Arabian oasis.

  • White Robata

    • This award winning restaurant will have you licking your lips before you've even sat down. If you love Asian fusion and are looking for a top fine dinning establishments, then stop looking because this is it.

  • Dar Hamad

    • This authentic traditional Kuwaiti restaurant should be a top contender if you are looking for a taste of Kuwait.

  • Ovo

    • This vegan/vegetarian restaurant will have you going back for more. With its spin on the Middle Eastern fare, it will have all your taste buds satisfied. This stand out one of a kind restaurant in Kuwait will make you sad to leave.

  • The Cheesecake Factory

    • As the name may give it away this restaurant provides an abundance of mouth mouth-watering cheesecakes, however before indulging in one of the many wonderful deserts, why not savour some of this American diners home-cooked foods.

  • Crowne Plaza Rib Eye

    • This steakhouse is perfect for the family, with its succulent food and excellent service, you'll be happy you went for a bite or two.
