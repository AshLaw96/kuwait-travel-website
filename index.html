Kuwait is a must country to visit in the Middle East. It might not be one of the most well-known travel destinations, but I'm going to show you why it should be a travel destination for you. With its beautiful architecture, stunning natural wonders, and modern Arabic feel, Kuwait is more than a desert haven.

Accommodations

There is an abundance of places to stay in Kuwait whether it's luxury you want or a family-oriented stay, here are just a few of them:

  • Waldorf Astoria Kuwait

    • The Waldorf has 200 lavish rooms and suites to choose from, with 5 mouth-watering restaurants, a luxurious spa, an outdoor pool, and an exclusive club. If fine dining and 5-star lodgings is what your looking for this may be the place for you!

  • Marriott Executive Apartments

    • The Marriott has one-, two-, and three-bedroom studio apartments, with your own kitchenette and dinning area. If you prefer a more homely like feel but with the 5-star standers then look no further than the Marriott!

  • Hilton Kuwait Resort

    • With a variety of comfortable rooms for all the family, or perhaps a villa with your own private pool. The Hilton Resort will be a perfect getaway for you to explore!

  • Sama Aljeyad

    • This entire home in Abdali includes everything you need for you and your family to enjoy. The Sama Aljeyad even has its own football pitch and many other incredible activities for you to make the most amazing memories!

  • Flowery Holiday Suites

    • This boutique accommodation has a variety of unique spacious rooms at a budget. The hotel has its very own terrace so you can view all the beautiful glimmering lights of Kuwait City at night. If your looking for a stay that is a little bit quirky but at a reasonable price, this is a perfect choice!
